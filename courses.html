B E Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering focuses on designing, constructing, and maintaining physical and naturally built environments. This program equips students with skills in structural analysis, geotechnics, transportation, and water resources. Graduates find roles in infrastructure projects, construction, urban planning, and environmental management. With the increasing demand for sustainable development, civil engineers are vital in shaping the future of cities and communities.

B E Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is the branch that deals with machines, materials, and energy systems. This program offers deep insight into thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, material science, and manufacturing processes. Graduates work in automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, and energy sectors. The versatility of mechanical engineers allows them to be involved in product design, R&D, and even management roles across industries.

B E Electronics & Communication Engineering

This course provides knowledge on electronics, signal processing, and communication systems. Students learn to design, develop, and test electronic devices like semiconductors, antennas, and integrated circuits. Graduates can work in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and data communication. With the rise of 5G and IoT, this field offers significant growth opportunities.

B E Computer Science and Engineering

This course prepares students in programming, algorithms, and data structures, focusing on software development and system design. Students learn about operating systems, databases, networking, and cybersecurity. Graduates can pursue careers in software engineering, data science, AI, and cloud computing. With tech innovation driving global progress, this is one of the most sought-after fields.

B E Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)

This specialized course integrates core Computer Science with AI technologies like machine learning, neural networks, and natural language processing. Students learn to build intelligent systems and solve complex problems. Graduates find roles in AI development, data analysis, and robotics. AI is reshaping industries globally, making this course a key to future technological leadership.

B E Electrical & Electronics Engineering

This course covers electrical power generation, distribution, and control systems, as well as electronic circuits and devices. Students gain expertise in electrical machines, power electronics, and automation. Graduates are employed in industries like power generation, renewable energy, and consumer electronics. With the shift towards sustainable energy solutions, this field is becoming increasingly important.

B E Automation & Robotics

This course focuses on the automation of processes using robotics and control systems. Students learn programming, control theory, and sensor integration to create efficient automated systems. Graduates work in industries like automotive manufacturing, robotics design, and industrial automation. The demand for automation solutions is rising as industries seek efficiency and precision.

B E Biotechnology

This program combines biology and engineering principles to address challenges in healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sectors. Students study genetic engineering, bioinformatics, and industrial bioprocesses. Graduates can work in pharmaceuticals, research, and agriculture. Biotechnology is a field with immense potential for solving critical global challenges.

B E Electronics Engineering (VLSI Design & Technology)

This course focuses on the design and fabrication of integrated circuits (ICs). Students learn digital design, semiconductor physics, and chip fabrication. Graduates find roles in IC design companies, semiconductor manufacturing, and research. VLSI is crucial to modern electronics, with applications in smartphones, computers, and IoT devices.

Bachelor of Architecture

This course trains students in designing buildings and other physical structures. It covers architectural design, building materials, and environmental sustainability. Graduates work in architecture firms, urban planning, and interior design. With a focus on sustainable development, architecture plays a vital role in shaping modern cities.
